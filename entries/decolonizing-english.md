# Decolonizing English

Broooooo  
how do I decolonize English?  
I don’t meant it in the warm and fuzzy way  
but like  
how can I pack John Brown and a big gun  
in a paragraph?  
How can poetry slam  
like a bullet through Custer’s skull  
fired from a European rifle  
atop horses that were extinct  
before the settler came?

To even say “decolonize” seems too soft  
like putting Legos back together,  
knocked over by a toddler we nicknamed  
the Pale Devil  
like it were cute.

Maybe it’s not to decolonize  
but to indigenize  
because to think of indigeneity  
as genetic legacy and land claims,  
bro that’s settler shit.  
Bro, blood and soil is Nazi shit.  
Take it from a German:  
*Blut und Boden ist Nazi Scheiße.*

If you hear me when I tell you  
indigeneity is not a matter of ancestry  
but of ways of relating and knowing,  
like knowing a continent as a comrade  
for hundreds of thousands of years  
past and future,  
like Spaceship Earth blasting off  
into time immemorial—  
if you only hear it when I say it,  
I wonder about all the times  
you weren’t listening.  
I wonder if I can trust you  
to hold the path open all the way  
from your ears to your heart.  
I wonder if you will sit with that.

When my friend says,  
“Socialism is all well and good  
but have you considered potlatch?”  
do I dump fifty thousands words on him  
that all mean YES COMRADE I HAVE  
or do I pick apart that he already thinks  
potlatch has nothing to do with socialism  
because obviously socialism is for settlers  
and Jose Carlos Mariategui  
must have just been flexing whiteness  
when he described indigenous subjugation  
as a matter of economic devastation,  
of race as a colonial construct,  
of borders as a wall except for capital,  
of the Indian as a category of exemption,  
of Westphalian land relation  
as decivilizing?  
Maybe to indigenize will be to civilize  
but when you hear a pale face saying  
“bro I want to civilize you”  
I would say you have a right to bear arms  
in self defense,  
so unless you and I make Quechua  
Turtle Island’s new lingua franca  
then how do we decolonize English  
so you understand who exactly  
I am really threatening?
